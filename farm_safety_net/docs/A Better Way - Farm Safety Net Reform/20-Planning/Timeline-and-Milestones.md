# Timeline and Milestones

## Introduction

This timeline establishes the four-year implementation schedule for A Better Way: Farm Safety Net Reform Initiative, from November 2025 through October 2029. The timeline coordinates the dual implementation of "No Farm Lost" crisis assistance and "Rethinking the Safety Net" policy development, aligning with the three-phase communications strategy and quarterly working group meetings. Key milestones track progress toward preventing farm loss, developing comprehensive safety net reforms, and building the strategic partnerships necessary for policy implementation.

## Success Metrics and Monitoring

Progress toward the initiative's goals will be monitored through quarterly assessments aligned with working group meetings, annual comprehensive evaluations, and continuous tracking of farmer assistance outcomes. Key performance indicators include farm loss prevention rates, policy adoption progress, partnership network growth, and farmer-to-farmer network sustainability. The timeline ensures regular milestone achievement while maintaining flexibility for strategic adjustments based on emerging opportunities and challenges in the agricultural policy landscape.
